We are pleased to invite you to The Pub on Pryor. We've made it a place where redactive imagination mixes with boolean pseudochildren of yesteryear. We think in terms of eons, not life cycles, and that's why we open our doors to you. Step across the thresh, pull down the board, and settle in. Look to your left and right, surrounded in the embrace of your fellow travelers. They welcome you; We welcome you ...to one of the city's special places.

When you think of Atlanta, you'll think of magnolias and origin branches, long sweet summer days and global undefined nights, but we think mostly, you'll think of home, which will lead you here. You'll know it when you walk in. You'll feel it when you're here. And you'll find you miss it when you're gone. So come and enjoy one of the city's new treasures....and "Welcome Home."

  • Our Story

    "We are drawn to the old world recipes of the new world order."-Jeff H

    We present an amalgam of esoteric optimism mixed with a barley/hops concatenation of drinking bliss. It's not pretentious or redundant. It's an homage to the full glass, the raised goblet, the lived life. And that... in a word, is why we are here. Sometimes you just know. And at a time and place like this, We Know...We grow...We pay...We play...and, we serve interpolated libations, without the different level of functions that are overwhelming or iterated.

    Borders...boundaries...Do they really exist? Or are they imposed on us? Do we push forward only to be pulled back? The existential crisis of humanity can be embraced in the lessons of the past...and sometimes, those lessons are found in a frosty mug, or in a warm hearth, or the smile of a friend.

    We are drawn to the old world recipes of the new world order. We are grounded in the past with an eye toward the future. We keep our feet on the ground while we reach for the stars. Come and join us. And raise a glass...and toast the promise of tomorrow while you enjoy the blessings of today.
